OUR PRODUCTS

Coir Fibre and Coco peat (Coir fibre pith)


* 100% Natural
* 100% Eco-friendly
* 100% Renewable

Two major products namely COIR FIBRE and COCO PEAT are extracted from the fibrous mesocarp or middle layer of the coconut fruit.







Coir Fibre

Coir fibre is extracted by soaking whole husk in water for about 6 weeks to loosen the fibre, then combed out sundried further processed according to the requirement of the end product.


In general coir fibre is available in following forms


• Coir mattress fibre (short and soft fibre)

• Coir mixed fibre (medium sized fibre)

• Coir bristle fibre in1-tie, 2-tie, 3-tie(long and strong fibre)

Coco Peat

Coco Peat is geneated while combing. Coconut husk is cut into specific sizes too without being combed according to the demand.


Coco peat is processed and available in following forms


• Brown coco peat (low EC and high EC with higher expansion)

• Black coco peat (Low EC and with lower expansion)

• Washed coco peat (low EC higher expansion)

• Buffered coco peat (Chemically washed to obtain low EC)
